# Biography

## Introduction

Prince Singh is an undergraduate engineering student from India with a strong passion for software engineering, backend development, artificial intelligence, and modern Large Language Model (LLM) applications. His learning journey reflects a gradual transition from traditional programming to advanced AI engineering, driven by curiosity and a desire to understand how systems work beneath the surface.

Unlike many learners who stop after completing tutorials, Prince consistently prefers building complete projects. This project-first mindset has shaped much of his technical growth, allowing him to gain practical experience with deployment, debugging, databases, networking, AI frameworks, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

Throughout his journey, he has demonstrated persistence, analytical thinking, and a willingness to spend considerable time solving difficult technical problems instead of abandoning them.
