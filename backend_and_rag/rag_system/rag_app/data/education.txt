# Self-Learning Approach

Prince rarely treats classroom instruction as the endpoint of learning.

Instead, his usual workflow is:

1. Learn the fundamentals.
2. Build a small implementation.
3. Expand it into a complete project.
4. Deploy it.
5. Debug real-world issues.
6. Optimize the implementation.
7. Understand the underlying architecture.

This iterative approach has contributed significantly to his technical growth.
